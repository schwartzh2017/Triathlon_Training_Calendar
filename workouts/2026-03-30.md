## Non-negotiables before you start

Collagen + tart cherry juice 60min before the swim.

**Eat real food between the swim and the lift.** Not just the collagen stack — actual food with carbohydrate and protein. 15–20min after finishing the swim, before you set up for lifting. A banana + Greek yogurt, rice + eggs, anything substantial. The swim and the lift are two separate demands on your body and you need fuel between them.

Shin check before lifting.

---

## Swim — 30min

**Goal:** Aerobic base, technique focus.

HR ceiling 155 bpm.

Suggested structure if no coached session:
- 200m easy warm-up
- 4×100m @ comfortable aerobic effort, 20s rest
- 4×50m slightly stronger, 15s rest
- 100m easy cool-down

Focus on catch position and lat engagement — this is where your pull-up work is starting to pay off.

## Sauna

Begin within 15min of finishing the lift.

10–12min sauna, then cold if available.

Collagen synthesis window — if you haven't had a second collagen dose, this is the time.

---

## Achilles monitoring protocol

After every run and after calf raises: 2 min calf stretch on a step.

Both straight-leg (gastrocnemius) and bent-knee (soleus).

Hold each 30s per side.

Report any tightness in Tuesday's log.

## Progression notes

Deadlift: whatever top set you hit today becomes the Week 4 anchor.

Single-leg RDL: if 42 lb felt stable and controlled, that's Week 4 working weight.

Split squat: same — if 42 lb felt clean, lock it in.

Glute bridge: if 50 lb felt RPE ≤ 8, move to 50 lb across all sets next Legs A.
