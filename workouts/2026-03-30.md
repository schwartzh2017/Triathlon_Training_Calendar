## Swim — 30min

**Goal:** Aerobic base, technique focus.

HR ceiling 155 bpm.

Suggested structure:
- 200m easy warm-up
- 4×100m @ comfortable aerobic effort, 20s rest
- 4×50m slightly stronger, 15s rest
- 100m easy cool-down

Focus on lat engagement and catch position — connect it to your pull-up work.
