## Run — Easy 30min

*Start this 10–15min after finishing the lift.*

**Goal:** Aerobic recovery run, not a fitness test.

**Target pace:** 10:00–11:00/mi — genuinely easy, not "easy but secretly pushing."

Wednesday's run showed your cardiovascular system is not yet calibrated to your leg fitness.

Today we deliberately run slower than feels necessary.

**HR ceiling:** 155 bpm.

If HR climbs above 155, slow to a walk until it drops back to 145, then resume.

**Shin check mid-run:** At the 15-min mark, note any shin sensation.

If anything feels off, walk the remainder.

**What success looks like today:** Finishing the 30 minutes with HR staying mostly aerobic and legs feeling okay.

Not pace. Not effort. Just completing a controlled aerobic stimulus on post-strength, post-long-ride legs.
