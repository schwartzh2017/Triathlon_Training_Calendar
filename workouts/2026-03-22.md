## 1. Main Lift — Bent Over Row (Underhand Grip, Barbell)
**4 sets · 2 min rest between sets**

*Small load increase on top set from Week 1 — only apply if Week 1 felt RPE 7 or below on set 4.*

| Set | Reps | Weight |
|-----|------|--------|
| 1 (warm-up) | 8 | 65 lb |
| 2 | 5 | 80 lb |
| 3 | 5 | 85 lb |
| 4 | 5 | 85 lb |

**Cues:** Hinge to ~45°, full scapular retraction at top, 2 sec eccentric.

**RPE target:** 8 on sets 2–4.

---

## 2. Swim Specific — Band Lat Pulldown (Standing)
**3 sets · 90 sec rest between sets**

| Set | Reps | Load |
|-----|------|------|
| 1 | 10 | Moderate band tension |
| 2 | 10 | Moderate band tension |
| 3 | 10 | Moderate band tension |

**Cues:** Shoulders down, elbows tracking back, 3 sec return. This is your swim catch pattern.

*Progress to heavier band when 3 × 10 feels RPE 6 or below.*

---

## 3. Accessory A — Reverse Fly (Dumbbell)
**3 sets · 90 sec rest between sets**

| Set | Reps | Weight (per hand) |
|-----|------|-------------------|
| 1 | 8 | 10 lb |
| 2 | 8 | 10 lb |
| 3 | 8 | 10 lb |

**Cues:** Slight bend in elbow, lead with elbows, pause 1 sec at top.

*Holding same weight as Week 1. Add 2 lb per hand in Week 3 if RPE is 6 or below.*

---

## 4. Accessory B — Bicep Curl (Dumbbell)
**3 sets · 90 sec rest between sets**

| Set | Reps | Weight (per hand) |
|-----|------|-------------------|
| 1 | 6 | 20 lb |
| 2 | 6 | 22 lb |
| 3 | 5 | 22 lb |

**Cues:** Alternate arms, full supination at top, 2 sec eccentric. No swinging.

*Small increase from Week 1 — top sets nudged up to 22 lb per hand.*

---

## 5. Abs — Kneeling Rollout + Hanging Knee Raise
**2 sets each · 60 sec rest after each pair**

### Kneeling Rollout (ab wheel)
- 2 sets · 10 reps

- *Brace hard before rolling. Lumbar neutral throughout.*

### Hanging Knee Raise (pull-up bar)
- 2 sets · 12 reps

- *Controlled, no swinging. Tuck pelvis at top.*

## Run Session
**Duration:** 30 min continuous

**Effort:** Conversational pace — Z2, RPE 4–5

**HR target:** Below 140 bpm throughout

*Second full 30min run. Same protocol as Wednesday — same easy pace, same shin monitoring checkpoints at 10 and 20 min.*

*If Wednesday's run produced any shin feedback, reduce today to 20 min and flag before adding more run volume.*

## Shin monitoring checkpoints
**At 10 min:** Any anterior shin sensation?

- None → continue

- Any point tenderness → stop, walk home

**At 20 min:** Same check.

- None → continue to 30 min

- Any new sensation → stop, walk home
