## Achilles Protocol — Mandatory Post-Run

Do this before contrast therapy, while muscles are still warm.

**Standing calf stretch on a step:**

*Straight-leg (gastrocnemius):*
- Stand with ball of foot on edge of step
- Lower heel below step level
- Hold 30s each side × 2 rounds

*Bent-knee (soleus):*
- Same position but with a slight bend in the knee
- Hold 30s each side × 2 rounds

Total: ~4 minutes.

Do not skip this.

Report in your log: did the achilles feel tight during the run, after, or not at all?
