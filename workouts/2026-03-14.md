## 1. Main Lift — Bent Over Row (Underhand Grip, Barbell)
**4 sets · 2 min rest between sets**

| Set | Reps | Weight |
|-----|------|--------|
| 1 (warm-up) | 8 | 65 lb |
| 2 | 5 | 80 lb |
| 3 | 5 | 80 lb |
| 4 | 5 | 85 lb |

**Cues:** Hinge to ~45°, full scapular retraction at top, 2 sec eccentric.
**RPE target:** 8 on sets 2–4.
*If set 4 at 85 lb feels like RPE 9+, hold 80 lb across all 3 working sets next week.*

---

## 2. Swim Specific — Band Lat Pulldown (Standing)
**3 sets · 90 sec rest between sets**

| Set | Reps | Load |
|-----|------|------|
| 1 | 10 | Moderate band tension |
| 2 | 10 | Moderate band tension |
| 3 | 10 | Moderate band tension |

**Setup:** Band anchored overhead. Pull to hips, squeeze lats at bottom, 3 sec return.
**Cues:** Shoulders down, elbows tracking back — not flaring wide. This is your swim catch pattern.
*Progress by using a heavier band, not adding reps.*

---

## 3. Accessory A — Reverse Fly (Dumbbell)
**3 sets · 90 sec rest between sets**

| Set | Reps | Weight (per hand) |
|-----|------|-------------------|
| 1 | 8 | 10 lb |
| 2 | 8 | 10 lb |
| 3 | 8 | 10 lb |

**Cues:** Hinged or seated. Slight bend in elbow, lead with elbows not hands, pause 1 sec at top.
*Critical for swim stroke longevity. Never rush these.*

---

## 4. Accessory B — Bicep Curl (Dumbbell)
**3 sets · 90 sec rest between sets**

| Set | Reps | Weight (per hand) |
|-----|------|-------------------|
| 1 | 6 | 18 lb |
| 2 | 6 | 20 lb |
| 3 | 5 | 22 lb |

**Cues:** Alternate arms, full supination at top, 2 sec eccentric. No swinging.

---

## 5. Abs — Kneeling Rollout + Hanging Knee Raise
**2 sets each · 60 sec rest after each pair**

### Kneeling Rollout (ab wheel)
- 2 sets · 10 reps
- *Brace hard before rolling. Only go as far as you can return without losing lumbar neutral.*

### Hanging Knee Raise (pull-up bar)
- 2 sets · 12 reps
- *Controlled, no swinging. Tuck pelvis at top.*

## Swim Note
The band pulldown and reverse fly are your two most swim-specific movements. If short on time, **cut biceps — never cut these two.**

---

## Progression Rules (Week 2+)
- Barbell row: add 5 lb when all 3 working sets feel RPE 7 or below
- Band pulldown: progress to heavier band when 3 × 10 feels RPE 6 or below
- Reverse fly: add 2 lb per hand when 3 × 8 feels RPE 7 or below
- Bicep curl: add 2 lb per hand when set 3 is completed at RPE 7 or below
