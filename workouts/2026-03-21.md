## Non-negotiables before you start
- Eat 20–30g carbohydrate within 60 min prior to plyometrics

- No fasted plyometrics

- 25–40g protein within 30–45 min after bike

- Minimum 30 min between end of plyometrics and start of bike

---

## Plyometrics Session
**Bilateral work:** 30 sec on / 15 sec rest

**Unilateral work:** 20 sec per side / no rest between sides / 20 sec rest between exercises

> **Shin rule:** Any anterior shin pain during any hop or bound = stop that exercise immediately.

> Do not push through tibial discomfort in plyometrics. The bone loading stimulus is valuable — the risk of overloading a recently healed tibia is not worth it.

---

### Bilateral Exercises

| Exercise | Work | Rest |
|---|---|---|
| Double hops in place | 30 sec | 15 sec |
| Forward/backward hops | 30 sec | 15 sec |
| Triple hops | 30 sec | 15 sec |
| Side to side hops | 30 sec | 15 sec |
| Scissor hops | 30 sec | 15 sec |
| Walk + hop alternating double/single | 30 sec | 15 sec |
| Box hop down + jump | 30 sec | 15 sec |

---

### Unilateral Exercises

| Exercise | Work per side | Rest |
|---|---|---|
| Single leg forward/backward hops | 20 sec each | 20 sec after both sides |
| Single leg triple hop | 20 sec each | 20 sec after both sides |
| Single leg side to side | 20 sec each | 20 sec after both sides |
| Single leg lateral bounds | 20 sec each | 20 sec after both sides |
| Single leg box hop downs | 20 sec each | 20 sec after both sides |

---

### Unilateral shin monitoring
Single leg work places full tibial load on one leg at a time.

Check in with your shins after each unilateral exercise — not just at the end.

Any point tenderness on the tibia = stop all remaining single leg work for today. Bilateral work is fine to continue.

---

## Bike Session (after plyometrics)
**Type:** Long / hilly

**Effort:** Z2 endurance — the legs have already done plyometric work. Do not push the hills hard today.

**Duration:** Your normal long ride distance

*The bike after plyometrics serves as active recovery for the legs — low cadence, easy effort, flush the lactic acid and keep moving.*
