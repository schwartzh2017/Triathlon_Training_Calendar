## Run — 25–30min Easy + First Strides

**This is the run progression trigger I've been watching for.**

Your last three runs have shown avg HR 146–150 at controlled pace with zero Threshold time.

You're ready for strides.

**First 20–25min:** Easy aerobic, HR ceiling 152 bpm.

Same controlled effort as previous runs — start slow, find your rhythm.

**Final 5min — 4 strides:**

A stride is a gradual acceleration over ~20 seconds to about 5K effort (not a sprint), then decelerate and walk 30–45s before the next one.

Not a full sprint — think 85–90% of max effort, smooth and controlled.

| Stride | Duration | Effort | Recovery |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 20s | 80% — ease into it | 45s walk |
| 2 | 20s | 85% | 40s walk |
| 3 | 20s | 85% | 40s walk |
| 4 | 20s | 85% | Done |

*If any shin sensation during strides: stop immediately.*

*If strides feel completely fine: report back and we add them permanently to Sunday runs.*

**Achilles protocol after the run:**

Standing calf stretch — straight-leg and bent-knee, 30s each side × 2 rounds.
