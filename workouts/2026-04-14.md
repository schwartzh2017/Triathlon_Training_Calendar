## Welcome to Race Prep bike work

Base phase Thursdays were sweet spot and tempo — IF 0.75–0.88, sustained aerobic effort.

Race Prep Tuesday bike sessions are threshold and above — IF 0.88–0.95, shorter harder intervals with meaningful recovery between.

The goal shifts from building your aerobic engine to training the system you'll actually use on race day.

A sprint triathlon bike leg is ridden at or just below threshold for the full 20km.

Today you train that.

---

## Workout Selection — Exact Criteria

**Step 1:** Open SYSTM library, filter by duration 45–55min.

**Step 2:** Check these numbers in the workout preview:

| Metric | Target | Reject if |
|---|---|---|
| IF (Intensity Factor) | 0.88–0.95 | Below 0.85 or above 0.97 |
| TSS | 50–70 | Above 75 |
| NM dots | 0–1 filled | 3+ filled |
| Workout focus | Threshold or FTP | Sweet spot, micro-intervals, sprint |

**Step 3:** Cadence target 85–90 rpm throughout.

If dropping below 80 rpm → switch to Level Mode immediately.

**Good options to search:**

- **The Wringer** — over-under threshold intervals, excellent Race Prep workout
- **Defender** — threshold blocks
- **Attacker** — FTP focused
- **Half Monty** — if you want to retest FTP (recommended — see note below)
- Any workout labeled "Threshold" or "FTP" in training focus

**Avoid:** Long Scream (too hard for week 1 Race Prep), any micro-interval or sprint focused workouts.

## FTP Retest Consideration

Your current FTP is set at 170W in SYSTM.

Based on your recent sessions — completing The Holy Week at 95% FTP with controlled cadence, handling Long Scream despite it being too hard — your actual FTP is likely 175–185W.

An accurate FTP makes all your Race Prep sessions more precise.

**Half Monty** is SYSTM's ramp test — 30 minutes, gives you both FTP and max aerobic power.

If you want to retest today instead of a threshold workout, Half Monty is the right choice.

If you'd rather do a structured threshold session first: do that today, retest next Tuesday.

Your call — either is valid.

## Contrast Therapy

Cold first — reduces acute inflammation from threshold effort.

- 2–3min cold
- 10min heat
- 2–3min cold to finish
