The MAP-heavy structure (short sharp efforts over a sustained FTP base) is a good variation from The Wretched's sustained FTP focus. Alternating between these two workout styles across weeks trains different aspects of your threshold system — The Wretched builds sustained FTP endurance, Rue The Day builds the ability to produce repeated hard efforts and recover quickly. Both are race-relevant.

If Rue The Day feels too unfamiliar or you want to build confidence with a known workout: repeat The Wretched. Aim to bump the FTP target to 170W for the full session rather than waiting 20 minutes to adjust like last week.
