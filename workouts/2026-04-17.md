## Context — Second Swim Session

This is your second pool session of the week.

Monday was the longer aerobic base session with drills.

Friday is slightly shorter but includes more structured interval work — building swim-specific fitness rather than just base volume.

The goal over the coming weeks is to develop the ability to swim 750m at race pace without blowing up before you hit the bike.

---

## Swim — 35min (~1,400 yards)

**Warm-up — 300 yards easy freestyle**

Easy, no pressure. Just get the feel of the water back after Monday.

**Drill Set — 150 yards**

3×50 yards with 20s rest:
- Length 1: Catch-up drill
- Length 2: Fingertip drag
- Length 3: Single-arm drill (one arm pulls while the other stays extended — trains high elbow and body rotation)

*Single-arm drill is new — keep it easy and focus on feel, not speed.*

**Main Set — 600 yards**

*Slightly more structured than Monday's main set.*

- 2×200 yards @ aerobic effort, 30s rest between
- Target pace: 1:48–1:55/100yd
- Focus on maintaining stroke count per length — try to keep it at 11 or below

**Speed Set — 250 yards**

- 5×50 yards at faster effort, 15s rest
- Target pace: 1:35–1:40/100yd
- This is your race-pace effort — uncomfortable but sustainable

**Cool-down — 100 yards easy**

*Total: ~1,400 yards*
