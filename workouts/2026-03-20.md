## 1. Main Lift — Conventional Deadlift (Barbell)
**5 sets · 3–4 min rest between sets**

*Weights below reflect progression from Week 1 top sets. Adjust based on your actual Week 1 RPE — if top sets felt RPE 9+, hold Week 1 weights.*

| Set | Reps | Weight |
|-----|------|--------|
| 1 (warm-up) | 8 | 95 lb |
| 2 | 5 | 140 lb |
| 3 | 5 | 165 lb |
| 4 | 4 | 170 lb |
| 5 | 4 | 170 lb |

**Cues:** Brace before pulling, push the floor away, hips and shoulders rise together, lockout tall.

**RPE target:** 8 on sets 3–5.

*Adding 5 lb to top sets from Week 1. If this feels like RPE 9+, note it and we hold here for Week 3.*

## Progression Rules (Week 3+)
- Deadlift: add 5 lb to top sets when RPE 7 or below on all working sets

- Single-leg RDL: add 2.5 lb per hand when all sets complete at RPE 7 or below

- Split squat: add 2.5 lb per hand when all sets complete at RPE 7 or below

- Glute bridge: add 5 lb when all sets complete at RPE 7 or below

- Calf raise: add 5 lb to top set only when RPE is below 7 — progress slowly
