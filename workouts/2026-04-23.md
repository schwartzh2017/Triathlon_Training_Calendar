## Workout Selection

**Option A — Recommended: The Wringer**

Over-under threshold intervals — alternates between just below FTP and just above FTP within each interval block.

This is the most effective threshold training format for endurance athletes. Forces your body to clear lactate while continuing to produce power — exactly what the race bike leg demands.

IF: ~0.88–0.92 | Duration: ~45min | TSS: ~55–65

**Option B: Defender**

Sustained threshold blocks — simpler structure, slightly less demanding than The Wringer.

IF: ~0.88–0.90 | Duration: ~45min | TSS: ~50–60

**Option C: Attacker**

FTP focused — longer sustained efforts.

IF: ~0.90–0.93 | Duration: ~45min | TSS: ~55–65

Any of these is correct. The Wringer is the best Race Prep choice if you're feeling good.
