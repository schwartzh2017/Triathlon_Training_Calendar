## Swim — 35min (~1,400 yards)

*Third swim session of the week — keep this controlled and aerobic.*

*Legs and arms will be carrying fatigue from the full lift above.*

**Warm-up — 300 yards easy freestyle**

**Drill Set — 150 yards**

3×50 yards with 20s rest:
- Catch-up drill
- Single-arm drill
- Fingertip drag

**Main Set — 600 yards**

3×200 yards @ comfortable aerobic effort, 30s rest.

Target pace: 1:48–1:55/100yd — slightly easier than Monday given cumulative fatigue.

Focus on stroke count and technique over pace today.

**Speed Set — 200 yards**

4×50 yards at faster effort, 15s rest.

Target pace: 1:35–1:40/100yd.

**Cool-down — 150 yards easy**

*Total: ~1,400 yards*

## Progression notes

Bench: density build — 3×5 at 85 lb is the goal before attempting 90 lb.

Arnold press: return to 60 lb top set.

Skull crusher: 5×45 lb target before moving to 50 lb.

Landmine: 6×30 lb target before progressing.

KB side bend: try 52.5 lb top set.

Single-leg hip thrust: note left/right difference.

Lateral lunge: progress to 25 lb each if 20 lb felt easy.
