## Swim — 35–40min (~1,550 yards)

**Warm-up — 400 yards easy freestyle**

**Drill Set — 200 yards**

4×50 yards alternating with 20–25s rest:
- Catch-up drill
- Fingertip drag
- Single-arm drill
- Catch-up drill

**Main Set — 600 yards — First 3×200yd**

*This is the progression from 7×100yd. Longer intervals train the sustained aerobic capacity you need for the 750m race swim.*

3×200 yards @ controlled aerobic effort, 30s rest between.

Target pace: 1:46–1:52/100yd.

*Pacing discipline is the key skill here — do not go out too fast on the first 200. Even splits across all three is the goal.*

Focus: stroke count at 11 or below per length.

If pace drops significantly on the third 200 (more than 8s/100yd slower): note the drop and we'll manage it.

**Speed Set — 200 yards**

4×50 yards at race pace effort, 15s rest.

Target pace: 1:33–1:38/100yd.

**Cool-down — 150 yards easy**

*Total: ~1,550 yards*
