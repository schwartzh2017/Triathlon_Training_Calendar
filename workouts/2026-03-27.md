Collagen + tart cherry juice 60min before you head out for the run.

## Run — Easy 20min

**This is a short, controlled aerobic stimulus — not a workout.**

Friday's run exists to maintain run frequency and support bone adaptation.

It is not a fitness session.

**HR ceiling: 150 bpm.**

Slightly lower ceiling than Wednesday because this run precedes a heavy strength session.

We are keeping the legs fresh for the deadlift.

**Pace:** Whatever keeps you under 150 HR.

Expect 10:30–11:30/mi.

Walk any hills if needed to stay under the ceiling.

**Duration:** 20min only.

Stop at 20 minutes regardless of how good you feel.

Saving the legs for the lift is the point.
