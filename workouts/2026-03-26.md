## Why this matters at Week 3

The Norwegian model builds aerobic capacity first — sustained sub-threshold work develops mitochondrial density and cardiac stroke volume.

Anaerobic capacity work (like SUF Idol) is valuable but comes later in the cycle when your aerobic base can absorb it.

Right now every Thursday should be leaving you slightly fatigued but not depleted.

If you finish the ride and feel like you couldn't do another 20 minutes, the intensity was too high.

If you finish and feel like you worked genuinely hard but have something left, that's the target.
