## Swim — 30min

**Goal:** Aerobic base.

HR ceiling 155 bpm throughout.

If you have a structured workout from your club or coach, use it.

If not, a simple structure that works well at this stage of base building:

- 200m easy warm-up, any stroke
- 4×100m @ comfortable aerobic effort, 20s rest between
- 4×50m slightly stronger effort, 15s rest between
- 100m easy cool-down

Total: ~1,100m.

Don't force pace.

The goal this week is consistent aerobic stimulus in the water, not speed.

## Sauna / Cold Contrast Therapy

Collagen synthesis window: if you haven't already had your post-workout collagen, this is a good time.

A standard contrast protocol for recovery:

- 10–12min sauna (heat)
- 2–3min cold (plunge or cold shower)
- Repeat 2–3 rounds if time allows

The contrast drives parasympathetic recovery and helps clear metabolic byproduct from both the swim and the lift.
