## Non-negotiables before you start
- Eat 20–30g carbohydrate within 60 min prior
- No fasted training
- 25–40g protein within 30–45 min after

## PT Warm-up — Psoas Activation
**Do before any loading · ~8 min**

| Exercise | Sets | Load |
|---|---|---|
| Psoas arm raises | 2 | Bodyweight |
| Psoas lean back | 2 | Bodyweight |
| Psoas hold — left | 2 | Hold per PT protocol |
| Psoas hold — right | 2 | Hold per PT protocol |

*Focus on ipsilateral hip flexor engagement. Full range on lean back — feel the stretch at end range before returning.*

---

## 1. Main Lift — Conventional Deadlift (Barbell)
**5 sets · 3–4 min rest between sets**

| Set | Reps | Weight |
|-----|------|--------|
| 1 (warm-up) | 10 | 95 lb |
| 2 | 6 | 135 lb |
| 3 | 5 | 155 lb |
| 4 | 5 | 165 lb |
| 5 | 4 | 165 lb |

**RPE target:** 8 on sets 3–5.
*If set 5 feels like RPE 9+, hold 165 lb next week rather than going up.*

---

## 2. Accessory A — Single-Leg RDL (Dumbbell)
**3 sets each side · 90 sec rest between sets**

| Set | Reps | Weight (per hand) |
|-----|------|-------------------|
| 1 | 5 | 35 lb |
| 2 | 5 | 35 lb |
| 3 | 5 | 35 lb |

*Complete all sets on left, then right. Maintain load at 35 lb per hand — this is a drop in reps from prior training to shift into strength range. Add load in Week 2 if RPE is below 7.*

---

## 3. Accessory B — Split Squat (Dumbbell)
**3 sets each side · 90 sec rest between sets**

| Set | Reps | Weight (per hand) |
|-----|------|-------------------|
| 1 | 5 | 30 lb |
| 2 | 5 | 30 lb |
| 3 | 5 | 30 lb |

*Complete all sets on left, then right. Controlled descent — 3 sec down. Full hip extension at top.*

---

## 4. Superset — Single-Leg Glute Bridge (L + R)
**3 sets · 60 sec rest after each L+R pair**

| Set | Reps | Load |
|-----|------|------|
| 1 | 6 each | Bodyweight |
| 2 | 6 each | Bodyweight |
| 3 | 6 each | Bodyweight |

*Left immediately into right, then rest. Add a 10 lb plate in Week 2 if 6 reps feels effortless.*

---

## 5. Calf Raise — Standing
**3 sets · 60 sec rest between sets**

| Set | Reps | Weight |
|-----|------|--------|
| 1 | 10 | 60 lb |
| 2 | 10 | 70 lb |
| 3 | 10 | 80 lb |

*Bilateral only. Slow eccentric — 3 sec down. Full range of motion.*

> **Shin rule:** Any anterior shin pain or point tenderness during or after = stop immediately, skip remaining sets, note it, and flag to your medical team before the next run session.

## Progression Rules (Week 2+)
- Deadlift: add 5–10 lb to top sets when sets 4–5 feel RPE 7 or below
- Single-leg RDL: add 5 lb per hand when all sets complete at RPE 7 or below
- Split squat: add 5 lb per hand when all sets complete at RPE 7 or below
- Glute bridge: add 10 lb plate when 3 × 6 feels like RPE 6 or below
- Calf raise: add 5–10 lb to top set when RPE is below 7
