## Progression notes

Back squat: whatever you hit today becomes Week 4 working weight.

Hip thrust: if 225 lb felt RPE ≤ 8, move top set to 235 lb next Legs B.

Lunge and step-up: hold these weights for one more week to reinforce the lower rep range before adding load.

---

## The bigger picture on Sunday sessions

Run + Legs B on Sunday will feel harder than any other day this week.

That is by design.

Sunday is your highest cumulative fatigue day — coming off Saturday's long bike.

Training in a slightly fatigued state on the lifts builds resilience and teaches your body to produce force when it's not fully fresh.

This is exactly what a triathlon demands in the final kilometers of the run leg.
