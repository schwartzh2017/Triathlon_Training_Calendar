## Skiing — Half Day

**Treat as active recovery / fun, not training.**

Skiing is quad-dominant unilateral loading — similar to step-ups and lunges in terms of muscle demand.

You've already done plyos and a lift this morning so your legs will be working on some fatigue.

Ski at a comfortable, enjoyable pace.

Don't push hard on the last runs of the day — fatigued legs on skis is when injuries happen.

Hydrate and fuel throughout.

---

## Progression notes

Row: if 95 lb top set felt RPE ≤ 8, move to 95 lb working set next Arms A.

Pull-ups: if all 3 sets of 5 felt controlled with the strong band, we discuss reducing band thickness next Arms A.

Reverse fly: if 17 lb felt solid, that's the new working weight.

Curls: if you hit 5 reps on the last set at 22 lb, move to 22/24 lb next Arms A.
