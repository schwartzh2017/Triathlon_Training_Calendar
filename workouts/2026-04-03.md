**Day:** Friday

**Estimated time:** 20min run + skiing half day (2–4 hrs)

**Session order:** Run → Skiing

---

## Non-negotiables before you start

Collagen + tart cherry juice 60min before the run.

Shin check before heading out.

Achilles check — note status before first step.

Fuel properly before skiing — you'll be burning significant energy across the full morning between the run and the slopes.

---

## Run — Easy 20min

**Short, controlled, aerobic only.**

Legs need to be as fresh as possible for skiing.

**HR ceiling: 148 bpm** — slightly lower than usual to protect leg freshness for the slopes.

Start slow, stay slow.

Walk any uphill sections where HR approaches the ceiling.

Stop at exactly 20 minutes regardless of how good you feel.

**Achilles protocol after the run:**

Standing calf stretch on step — straight-leg and bent-knee, 30s each side × 2 rounds before heading to ski.

---

## Skiing — Half Day

**Treat as the main session today — this is meaningful lower body load.**

You've already run this morning so legs are pre-fatigued.

Ski at a comfortable, enjoyable pace throughout.

Don't push hard on the last runs — fatigued legs on skis is when injuries happen.

Hydrate and fuel throughout the ski day.

No plyos, no lift today — correct call given skiing is already providing significant unilateral quad and glute loading.

---

## What to report back

Achilles status during and after the run.

How legs felt skiing relative to previous ski days.
