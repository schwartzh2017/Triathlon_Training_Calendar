## Workout Selection — Exact Criteria

**Step 1:** Open SYSTM library and filter by duration 45–55min.

**Step 2:** Check these numbers in the workout preview before selecting:

| Metric | Target | Reject if |
|---|---|---|
| IF (Intensity Factor) | 0.75–0.85 | Above 0.88 |
| TSS | 40–55 | Above 60 |
| NM dots | 0–1 filled | 3+ filled |
| AC dots | 0–1 filled | 3+ filled |
| Workout focus | Sweet spot or Tempo | Micro-intervals, Attack, HIIT, Scream |

**Step 3:** Cadence target 85–90 rpm throughout.

If you drop below 80 rpm to hold watts → switch to Level Mode.

**Good options to search:**

- The Defender
- Half is Easy
- Tempo (any labeled tempo)
- Joyride
- Anything labeled "Base" in training focus

**Do not pick:** Long Scream, any Scream variant, Violator, The Shovel, or any workout rated "Extremely Hard."
