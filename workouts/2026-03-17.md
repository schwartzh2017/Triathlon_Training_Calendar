## Rest Day

Full rest.

No training today.

*Rest is a training variable. This is where bone remodeling, muscular repair, and neuromuscular adaptation actually happen — not in the gym.*

---

## Non-negotiables on rest days
- Hit your protein target — 25–40g per meal, minimum 3 meals

- Prioritize sleep — 7–9 hours. This is non-negotiable for RED-S recovery and bone health.

- Stay hydrated

- Light walking is fine. Nothing that elevates HR meaningfully.

---

## Whoop check
If your recovery score is below 50% this morning, consider whether Wednesday's first full 30min run should be pushed to Thursday and the bike moved to Wednesday instead.

*A suppressed HRV on the day before your first full run is worth paying attention to.*
