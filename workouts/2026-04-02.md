## This week's specific focus — cadence

Last week your avg cadence dropped from 86 rpm (December) to 82 rpm under fatigue.

This week try to maintain **85–90 rpm** throughout the workout, even when it gets hard.

High cadence at sub-threshold power reduces muscular fatigue and trains the pedaling efficiency you'll need on race day.

If ERG mode is fighting you on cadence, switch to Level Mode for the intervals.
