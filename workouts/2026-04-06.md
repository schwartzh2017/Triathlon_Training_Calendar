## Swim — 35min (~1,500 yards)

*Extended from last week's 27min / 1,200 yards — first step in building swim volume.*

**Warm-up — 400 yards easy freestyle**

**Drill Set — 200 yards**

*New addition. Focus on one drill per length, alternating:*

- **Catch-up drill:** One arm stays extended forward until the other hand catches up to it before pulling. Trains high elbow catch and lat engagement — directly connects to pull-up work.
- **Fingertip drag:** On recovery, drag fingertips along the surface of the water. Trains high elbow recovery and reduces crossover.

Do 4×50 yards alternating between the two drills. Easy effort, focus on feel not speed.

**Main Set — 600 yards**
- 4×100 yards @ comfortable aerobic effort, 20s rest
- Target pace: 1:45–1:50/100yd

**Speed Set — 200 yards**
- 4×50 yards slightly stronger effort, 15s rest
- Target pace: 1:35–1:40/100yd

**Cool-down — 100 yards easy**

*Total: ~1,500 yards*
