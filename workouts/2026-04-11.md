## Bike — Hill Repeats

**Context:** Friday's group ride was 96% recovery zone, avg HR 114 — essentially a social spin. Today compensates with meaningful aerobic stimulus.

**Ride to the climb — easy**

~5 miles, 15–20 min at recovery effort.

HR ceiling 138 bpm on the way there — this is your warm-up.

---

**Hill Repeats — 3 rounds (add a 4th if feeling strong after round 3)**

Each repeat:

- Climb at genuine aerobic effort — push into Aerobic Endurance and Aerobic Power zones (HR 138–164 bpm)
- Descend easy — full recovery before the next repeat
- Rest at the bottom until HR drops back below 130 bpm before starting the next climb

| Repeat | Target effort | Target HR on climb |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Moderate — find your legs | 138–155 bpm |
| 2 | Strong aerobic effort | 145–164 bpm |
| 3 | Same as repeat 2 | 145–164 bpm |
| 4 (optional) | Only if RPE still feels easy after 3 | 145–164 bpm |

Stop at 3 repeats if legs feel heavy or HR climbs higher than expected across repeats.

Stop at 4 repeats regardless — plyos and Legs B follow.

**Ride home — easy**

~5 miles back at recovery effort. HR back below 138 bpm.

**Target bike metrics:**
- Duration: 75–100min | Elevation: 800–1,200 ft | Avg HR: 130–145 | TL: 100–150
